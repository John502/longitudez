# Quick Fridge Pickle Brine

Little brine recipie I have been using for fridge pickles.

I have used these both for chip slices and a relish. 

I like to through some red onions in to give it that pinkish color. 

```bash
1 cup water
1 & 1/3 cup vinegar
1/3 cup sugar
2 Tbsp kosher salt
```
