# Tuna Steak Marinade 

Makes enough for a couple steaks. 

```
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup orange juice
2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
1 Minced Garlic Clove
1/2 Tsp Oregano
1/2 Tsp Black Pepper
```
